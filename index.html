Cornell Club of Taiwan

CCT is an on-campus organization; we cater to the diverse interests of the Taiwanese community.

We pursue finance, engineering, technology, medical, and many other careers.

We are building a platform for Taiwanese Cornellians to grow, network, and also give back.

We are a home away from home.
