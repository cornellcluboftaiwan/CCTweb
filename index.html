What We Do

Learn

Learn the basics of cardistry, object manipulation, and sleight of hand magic.

If you've ever seen an effect and thought "I want to do that", now's your chance to learn! We'll start off with the very basics, and with the right amount of practice and dedication. you will be able to perform for family, friends, and if you dare - a live audience.

Create

Design new effects by discussing magic with fellow aficionados.

While learning techniques is always interesting, creating a new technique is something entirely different. If you've ever had an idea for a new technique, this is the place where you can meet with others to turn your ideas into astonishing illusions.

Impress

Perform anywhere to bring the wonders of magic to your audience.

The point of magic and cardistry is to bring a sense of wonder into the lives of others, so take some time to go out and perform! We provide opportunities for you to perform in front of peers, on stage, and even on the streets and commons of Ithaca.

About Us

Once upon a time, in a land far far away, there lived five magicians, with powers beyond comprehension.


While we'd like to believe that's where our club originated, it isn't.

CSM is simply a club for people interested in either learning or practicing card manipulation. For people who are new to these topics, CSM offers a place to learn the basics of card magic, cardistry, and probability. For those who already have a background in the magical arts, CSM provides a place where practitioners can gather to create new effects or improve old ones.
